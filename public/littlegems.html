A LITTLE GAME

Match the feeling to the moment.

A gentle game for children — pair each feeling with the moment that gave it, and collect a gem for every match. A small way to put words to big feelings.

ANOTHER LITTLE GAME

Choose the kindest thing to do.

Read a little moment, then tap the gem holding the kindest choice. A gentle way to practice empathy and good decisions — and every kind choice adds a gem to the same jar.

A CALMING GAME

Breathe, and help a gem feel calm.

Pick a gem, then breathe along slowly as it grows and shrinks. Watch it cool from a big feeling to calm — a gentle way to settle a racing heart. Each calm breath adds a gem to the same jar.

WAYS TO USE THEM

Small moments, tenderly held.

Give to a grandparent
A finished Gem becomes a small letter from a child — a moment they made just for someone they love.
Tuck into a memory box
Keep a child's Gems somewhere safe. Years later, their voice in those drawings will sound exactly like who they were.
Share them as conversation
Sit with a child and a Gem. Ask the questions out loud. Let them tell you about the people and moments they love.
