What is a Gem

A Gem is a moment worth keeping.

A Gem holds something other forms of memory let slip — not only what happened, but who someone is, and how it feels to be near them.

Why Voice

Voice carries what words alone cannot.

A familiar voice can reach someone long after other things begin to fade. It holds identity, emotion, and belonging in the same breath.

Identity

Who we are

Voice carries the self — accent, rhythm, humor, history. One of the most personal signatures a person leaves.

Emotion

How we feel

Tone reaches places text never does. Warmth and reassurance travel in the sound, not just the words.

Belonging

Where we connect

A loved one’s voice signals safety and closeness — the feeling of being known, and of being home.

Why We Built MemoriGems

Built by people who have been in the room.

Lisette Diaz Santiago

Co-founder

More than 14 years across healthcare and education — including over a decade inside one of the country’s largest safety-net health systems. At Boston Medical Center her work moved from coordinating a high-acuity surgical ICU to leading patient-experience initiatives and designing system-wide training as Learning & Development Manager. Her path to MemoriGems is also personal: supporting her grandfather through progressive dementia, she witnessed that while memories may change, meaningful connection can remain.

E

Edwina Castone

Expertise in learning, family engagement, relationship-centered support, and communication — helping people navigate meaningful life experiences through connection and belonging. She brings the conviction that people deserve to feel known and valued at every stage of life.

Together, we share one belief: technology should help people feel known, connected, valued, and remembered.
